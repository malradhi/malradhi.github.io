About

Hi, and welcome! I got a BSc degree in Computer Engineering at Basra University in 2007, and a MSc degree in Systems Engineering at Portsmouth University, UK which was achieved with first honors in 2012 and awarded the MSc top student certificate in 2013. I started from September 2016 to complete my PhD at the Speech Technology and Intelligent Interactions Laboratory in the Budapest University of Technology and Economics. I am working on designing vocoders and acoustic models for statistical speech synthesis. My main interest are the signal speech processing, acoustic models, voice conversion, deep learning, and machine lerning.
